Bootcamp Prework Week 4

What was one thing you liked and disliked about each of the new topics covered in Pre Work?

Html
Like: The simplicity
Dislike: Nothing
CSS
Like: How it adds personality to your website
Dislike: Spacing and Padding
JavaScript
Like: The possibilities a power
Dislike: The syntax

What was helpful?

During the prework phase of bootcamp the daily meetings and interactions with the cohort were the most helpful for me. The Slack help channel and daily stand up are great ways to learn from others and voice your struggles.

What hindered your progress?

I believe my learning style has hindered my progress. A lot of our material is reading. I am better suited to learn from visual/tactile methods.

How did you overcome challenges?

“If at first you don’t succeed, try, try again”
In this program it is essential to have a thick skin. You are going to fail every single day. It’s those of us who pick ourselves back up quick and hop back on the horse who succeed.
